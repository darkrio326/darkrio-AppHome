Capture

A record starts wherever the expense appears.

Import a payment screenshot, photograph a receipt, speak one sentence, share from another app, or run a Shortcut. AutoLedger extracts a draft and keeps you in control of the final record.

01

Screenshots and receipts

On-device OCR and parsing recognize amount, merchant, time, and useful context from the material you choose.

02

Voice and natural language

Say a simple expense, confirm the parsed result, and keep moving. Local intelligence is used when available.

03

Shortcuts and sharing

Use the Action Button, Shortcuts, App Intents, clipboard, camera, or Share Extension to remove repeated steps.

Organize and understand

More than an inbox for expenses.

Multi-ledger organization, merchant aliases, categories, subscriptions, advanced search, cleanup tools, monthly reports, and export turn captured records into a usable financial memory.

01

Multiple ledgers

Separate personal, travel, work, or family records while keeping capture fast.

02

Reports that explain

See categories, trends, frequent merchants, subscriptions, and month-to-month changes.

03

Clean up in batches

Find duplicates, normalize merchants, apply confirmed rules, and review cloud-assisted suggestions before using them.

Privacy by boundary

Local-first, not cloud-blind.

Core recognition and ledger work begin on your device. Networked features are optional, purpose-specific, and documented instead of hidden behind an absolute promise.

On device

OCR, parsing, classification, deduplication, reports, and ledger editing are designed around local processing.

When you opt in

iCloud sync, the Pro folio inbox, Common API services, entitlement checks, and anonymous diagnostics use their specific cloud paths.

Your control

Review drafts before saving, manage permissions, turn off optional services, export records, or delete local data.

Spend your attention on the month, not every entry.
